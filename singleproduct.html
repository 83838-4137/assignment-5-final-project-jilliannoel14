Sunfinity Sunflowers

Sunflowers that are grown with care can brighten up your beloved home with a fun pop of yellow. These thrive and bloom continuously all season long and get up to between 3 to 4 feet high. They are low maintenance and drought tolerant flowers that grow in most soil conditions.

Flower Care Steps

Pot Care

  • Water when the top 2 inches of soil are dry, 2-3 times per week.
  • Drench the soil until water runs through the pot.

    • Ground Care

  • Water deeply but infrequently to encourage the plant to grow deep roots with several gallons of water per plant, more often if the weather is very dry or very hot.
  • May need support so they don’t topple under the weight of their blooms. Bamboo or other types of wood stakes work well.

    • Bouquet Care

  • Cut the thick stems at an angle.
  • Immediately place the flowers in a vase of tepid to cool water that has been prepped with a floral preservative. Tip: One teaspoon of sugar to two tablespoons of lemon juice or one tablespoon of vinegar to keep fresh.
  • Change the water every other day - more often if the water turns murky. Clean the vase before refilling with new water to remove any bacteria and trim the stems.
