Flower Care Steps

Pot Care
  • Water when the top 2 inches of soil are dry, 2-3 times per week.
  • Drench the soil until water runs through the pot.
    • Ground Care
  • Water deeply but infrequently to encourage the plant to grow deep roots with several gallons of water per plant, more often if the weather is very dry or very hot.
