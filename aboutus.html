Who We Are

Rebloom’s sells locally grown, organic flowers that are environmentally friendly as you can get. Our products go beyond the living flower as we prioritize ourself on repurposing the wilted flower so that nothing goes to waste. We have multiple store locations throughout the U.S. only.As a customer, you get to make a difference as much as the seller, becoming part of a team. You are buying with a purpose that makes earth a greener place to live while protecting the community of people that get directly impacted by Rebloom’s.

Meet the Founder

Leslie Miller started out loving to pick flowers from a young age to eventually buying them often at her local grocery store. An avid environmentalist, she started experimenting once the flowers died by drying them out to create extravagant bouquets, candles, and artwork. Passionate to keep the world as eco-friendly as possible, she opened Reblooms to honor our beautiful green world and make the most of each flower no matter its stage of life.
